So, what is this software apprenticeship...?

- my job is full time learning - no client code - pet projects as a fulltime task - 2 mentors, give me tasks and review my progress - continuous code reviews - duration depends on individual's progress (8 months)

- kata = small coding problem, which you solve with TDD - comes from martial arts - sequence of movements that you do over and over - you mostly know how to solve it - it's not about solving the problem but how you get to an elegant solution

What I learnt from katas

- goal: performance or screen recording with time restriction - practising over and over - improve my vim skills (finding shortcuts, typing) - TDD thinking become ingrained - certain refactorings went into my muscle memory (e.g. if/else to while to for) - live coding - nerve wracking - good to practise it
